Mission

We reveal numbers that can't be unseen. At the Eviction Research Network, we collect, analyze, and map eviction data. work to build a true network of partnerships with organizations working on the frontlines of housing justice.

  • Make eviction data from sources such as law offices and sherrif departments usable for analysis. That means organizing, structuring, and shedding duplicate or incomplete data.
  • Anayze eviction data for disparities in race, sex, gender, location, and other factors.
  • Map our analysis to advocate for policy change and real world impact.
